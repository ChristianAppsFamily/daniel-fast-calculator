Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your fasting data stays entirely on your device.

Introduction

Christian App Empire LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Daniel Fast Calculator mobile application ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in complete privacy. The Daniel Fast Calculator does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device identifiers (except as noted below for advertising)
  • Any data from your fasting records

Data Storage

All your fasting data, including:

  • Fasting start and end times
  • Fasting duration history
  • Fasting types selected
  • App settings and preferences
  • Reminder configurations

is stored locally on your device only using your device's secure storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

While we do not collect your data directly, the App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • General location (country/region level)

This data is used to serve relevant advertisements and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Using the App Tracking Transparency prompt when first launching the App (iOS)
  • Adjusting your device settings to limit ad tracking
  • Purchasing the "Remove Ads" option within the App

Apple StoreKit

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases (the "Remove Ads" feature). Apple processes all payment information. We do not have access to your payment details. Purchases are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you fasting completion reminders. You can disable this in your device settings.
  • App Tracking (iOS): To enable personalized advertising. This is optional and can be denied.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible within the App.
  • Delete your data: You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • Opt out of ads: Purchase the "Remove Ads" feature or adjust your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
