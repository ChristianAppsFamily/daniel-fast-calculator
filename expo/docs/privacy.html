Privacy Policy

Daniel Fast Calculator

1. Introduction

Christian App Empire LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Daniel Fast Calculator mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Local Data Only: The Daniel Fast Calculator stores all your fasting data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Fasting start and end times
  • Fasting duration settings
  • Fasting type preferences
  • App settings (theme, time format, notification preferences)
  • Purchase status for ad removal

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not use your information for any purpose. The App uses local data solely to:

  • Calculate fasting end times
  • Display your fasting history
  • Send local notifications for fasting reminders (if enabled)
  • Track ad removal purchase status

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using your device's secure local storage (AsyncStorage). We do not operate any servers or cloud services for this App.

Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): We display advertisements using Google Mobile Ads. AdMob may collect and use device information (such as device ID, IP address, and usage data) to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads by using the App Tracking Transparency controls on iOS or through your device settings.

6. In-App Purchases

The App offers an in-app purchase to remove advertisements for $4.99. This purchase is processed through Apple's App Store (StoreKit). We do not collect or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.

Purchase verification is performed through Apple's servers. We only receive confirmation of whether the purchase was successful.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will assist in removing such information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
